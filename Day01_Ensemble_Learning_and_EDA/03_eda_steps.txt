======================================
EDA (Exploratory Data Analysis) Steps
======================================
- Follow these standard steps before modeling:

    ================
    - Basic Checks:
    ================
        > Check shape, columns, data types.
        > Look for missing values.
        > Generate statistical summary (mean,std,min-max).

    ======================
    - Univariate Analysis:
    ======================
        > Plot histogram of numerical columns.
        > Check distributions.
        > Detect Outliers.

    =====================
    - Bivariate Analysis:
    =====================
        > Scatter plots.
        > Box plots.
        > Group-by comparisons.

    ============================
    - Target Variable Analysis:
    ============================
        > Countplot of the target (class distribution).
        > Identify imbalance if any.

    ========================
    - Correlation Analysis:
    ========================
        > Correlation matrix.
        > Heatmap to visualize relationships.
        > Identify multicollinearity.
